CASSIA Cell Type Annotation Summary (Breadth-First Analysis)

Single-cell RNA-seq Analysis Report

Overview

This report summarizes the iterative gene expression analysis performed by CASSIA to annotate a specific cell cluster. The analysis involved one primary evaluation phase, followed by a single iteration of gene expression checks. A total of 19 genes were examined across all phases.

Initial Assessment

The initial hypothesis was that the cell cluster represented IgA+ mucosal plasma cells, supported by strong expression of IgA-related genes (IGHA1, IGHA2, JCHAIN) and plasma cell markers (MZB1, DERL3, TNFRSF17). However, the presence of weak signals for FCRL5 and LILRB4 suggested potential minor memory B-cell contamination, and the absence of canonical plasma cell markers (CD138/SDC1, CD38) was identified as a validation gap. Low-ranking non-B-cell markers (MYL2, BMP6) were noted as potential noise or rare doublets.

Final Annotation

The cluster is confidently annotated as **IgA+ mucosal plasma cells** (high confidence). This annotation is strongly supported by the expression of IGHA1, IGHA2, JCHAIN, MZB1, DERL3, and the validated expression of SDC1, CD38, and PRDM1. The absence of CD19 and MS4A1 further confirms this cell type, indicating that the weak FCRL5 and LILRB4 signals were likely background noise.

Key Marker Genes

**IgA-related:** IGHA1, IGHA2, JCHAIN **Plasma Cell Markers (Canonical & Functional):** MZB1, DERL3, TNFRSF17, SDC1, CD38, PRDM1, XBP1, IRF4 **B-cell Markers (Negative):** MS4A1, CD19 **Memory B-cell Markers (Weak/Noise):** FCRL5, LILRB4 **Other (Low-ranking/Noise):** MYL2, BMP6, CCND2

Recommendations

No further action is needed unless investigating rare subpopulations within the cluster.
