Initial Assessment

The initial cell type hypothesis was Enteric Glial Cells (EGCs), supported by glial markers such as *SOX10, PLP1*, and *GFRA2*. However, the co-expression of neuronal markers (*NRXN1/3, L1CAM, SYT10*) introduced ambiguity, suggesting possibilities of a minor neuronal subpopulation, glial-neuronal transitional states, or doublets. The absence of canonical pan-neuronal markers (*ELAVL3, RBFOX3*) was noted as evidence against major neuronal contamination.
