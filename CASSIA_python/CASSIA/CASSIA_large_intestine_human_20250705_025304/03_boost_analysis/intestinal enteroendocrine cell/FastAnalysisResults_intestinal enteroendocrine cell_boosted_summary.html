CASSIA Cell Type Annotation Summary (Breadth-First Analysis)

Single-cell RNA-seq Analysis Report

Overview

This report summarizes the iterative gene expression analysis performed by CASSIA to annotate a specific cell cluster. The analysis involved one main evaluation phase, examining a total of 14 genes.

Initial Assessment

The initial hypothesis was that the cells were L-cell enteroendocrine cells (EECs). This was supported by the presence of canonical markers such as *GCG*, *PYY*, *UCN3*, *INSL5*, and neuroendocrine machinery markers *CHGA* and *PCSK1*. However, low-level expression of *SCT* and *TPH1* suggested potential minor subpopulations or transitional states, prompting further investigation.

Iteration 1

Hypotheses

1. To confirm the specificity of L-cell identity by checking for markers of other enteroendocrine cell types (K-cells, D-cells).
2. To assess potential contamination by other EEC subtypes (N-cells, G-cells, I-cells).
3. To clarify the identity of the *TPH1+* subset and check for transitional or immature states.

Genes Checked

GIPRPDX1SSTNTSGASTCCKDDCFEVASCL1

Key Findings

Expression analysis confirmed the absence or very low expression of *GIPR*, *PDX1*, and *SST*, supporting the L-cell identity and ruling out significant K-cell or D-cell contamination. Similarly, *NTS*, *GAST*, and *CCK* showed negligible expression, indicating a high purity of L-cells without significant contamination from N-cells, G-cells, or I-cells. Low expression of *DDC* and *FEV* suggested a minor serotonin-producing subset, consistent with the initial *TPH1* observation, but not indicative of a distinct major population. *ASCL1* expression was also low, ruling out a significant presence of immature or transitional states.

Final Annotation

The cluster is confidently annotated as **L-cell enteroendocrine cells (EECs)** of the human large intestine (**high confidence**). This conclusion is strongly supported by the dominant expression of canonical L-cell markers and neuroendocrine machinery, coupled with the negligible expression of markers for alternative EEC subtypes.

Key Marker Genes

**Canonical L-cell Markers:** *GCG*, *PYY*, *UCN3*, *INSL5* **Neuroendocrine Machinery:** *CHGA*, *PCSK1* **Minor Subpopulation Indicators:** *TPH1*, *DDC*, *FEV*

Recommendations

For further refinement, spatial transcriptomics or single-cell proteomics could be employed to resolve the localization of specific L-cell subtypes or the minor serotonin-producing subset.
