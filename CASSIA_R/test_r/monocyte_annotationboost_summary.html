Key Findings

High expression of ERBB3, the receptor for NRG1 and crucial for Schwann cell development and myelination, was detected, further strengthening the Myelinating Schwann Cell hypothesis. CD276 expression was also observed, which is not inconsistent with Schwann cells. DHH, NRG1, and CD56 were not found in the differential expression list, which did not contradict the Myelinating Schwann Cell identification. This iteration provided strong additional support for the Myelinating Schwann Cell annotation.

Final Annotation
